About Me

I am Doruk Alp Uzunarslan -I suppose you know that by now- and I live in Istanbul, Turkey. I am currently 14 years old. I am a student in Üsküdar Amerikan Lisesi (also known as Uskudar American Academy). I also have graduated from T3 (Türkiye Teknoloji Takımı [Turkey Technology Team] : An instuition that aims 'brightest' students from Turkey to gather in one place) and still going to BILSEM (Bilim ve Sanat Merkezi [Science and Art Center]: A project by the Ministry of Education where only some students who have shown significantly higher thinking capabilities than normal students are allowed to enter). Throughout my life, I have tried to attend as many competitions and festivals as I can. You can see the list of my achievements from here.

I am highly engaged with science and technology. I know everyone says that but I MEAN it. I have worked with amazing university teachers such as Bogazici University, Acibadem University, Marmara University and many more even though I am in high school. I have written many projects to TUBITAK and Teknofest as I had mentioned in my Qualifications page. I am always open for new things and projects so contact me through My Contacts if you have any questions or offers!
